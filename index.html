    About Us



    Tooniverse is a South Korean cable TV channel launched in 2000,
    specializing in animated content for all age groups. It broadcasts
    a wide variety of cartoons, anime, and animated series, including
    both domestic Korean productions and international imports from Japan,
    the U.S., and other countries. Tooniverse has gained popularity for
    airing well-known series such as *Pokémon*, *Dragon Ball Z*, and local
    Korean cartoons like *Pororo the Little Penguin*. It targets children
    and young audiences but also offers content suitable for older viewers.
    The channel is a key player in the South Korean animation industry and
    a go-to source for animated entertainment.
